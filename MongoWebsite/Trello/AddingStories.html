Adding Stories And Tasks

Stories in Trello are known as "Cards".

Cards are initially added to a list by clicking "Add a card..." on the desired List.

Once added thre are numerous actions items that can be added to a story:

  • - You can assign a members or multiple members to a Card
  • - Can be added to help categories Cards with coloured name tags. This is similar to our card colour conventions
  • - You can add a checklist to detail the tasks that need to be completed as part of the Card
  • - A due date can be associated to the Card
  • - Various items can be attached to the Card

By adding a Checklist to a Card you will be able to track progress of the overall card as the tasks associated are completed

Cards can be linked to associated cards in the Activity section

Notes can also be added to Cards from the Activity section. This could be used for checkin notes
